Meet HULKs

Become a HULK

You are interested in humanoid robotics, image processing, or artificial intelligence? You want to work on interesting research topics in a team of highly motivated students? You want to participate in challenging competitions all over the world?

If you can answer these questions with "Yes!" and want to help us bring our project to the next level, write a brief application in English or German to hulks@tuhh.de

To take a peek into the problems we are facing, check out our recruiting page and try to solve some of the tasks.
